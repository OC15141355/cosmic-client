## Detailed Progression

### Levels 1-30: Victoria Island

Start on Maple Island, get to Victoria Island. Pick your Explorer class at level 10:

| Class | Trainer | 2nd Job (Lv 30) |
|-------|---------|-----------------|
| Warrior | Perion | Fighter, Page, or Spearman |
| Mage | Ellinia | Fire/Poison, Ice/Lightning, or Cleric |
| Archer | Henesys | Hunter or Crossbowman |
| Thief | Kerning City | Assassin or Bandit |
| Pirate | Nautilus | Brawler or Gunslinger |

**Where to grind**:
- 1-10: Snails, Blue Snails, Shrooms (Maple Island)
- 10-20: Pigs, Ribbon Pigs, Orange Mushrooms (Henesys)
- 20-30: Wild Boars, Fire Boars, Zombie Mushrooms

**Don't miss**:
- Visit all 6 Vic towns → "Explorer of Victoria" medal + basic adventurer set from vendor
- **KPQ** (Kerning PQ, Lv 21-30, 4 players) — THE classic party quest. Do it.

### Levels 20-50: Ereve + Mushroom Castle

Two critical early quest chains:

**Ereve** (Lv 20-50): Take the boat from Ellinia. Complete all 5 CK commander quests + audience with Empress. Rewards: Knight Trainee gloves, Oz's Flame Pendant (+3 INT/LUK), weapon scroll, chairs, and the "Ereve Honored Guest" medal. **Do this.** These NPCs become enemies later and it hits different if you knew them.

**Mushroom Castle** (Lv 30-50): Finish the King Pepe questline. Reward: vendor unlocks selling **Maple Weapons** (Lv 35-64, every class). These are your weapons until Zipangu/Magatia. Iconic items, earned through a charming quest.

### Levels 30-70: The Wide World

This is where the world opens up. Multiple zones to explore, each with a completion vendor:

| Zone | What You Get | How |
|------|-------------|-----|
| **LPQ** (Ludi, Lv 35-50) | PQ rewards, EXP | Party quest, 3-6 players |
| **Ellin Forest** (Lv 45-55) | Lv 50 earring + ring (+3 all stats each) | Finish Ellin questline |
| **Omega Sector** (Lv 45-65) | Lv 55 hat + overall (sci-fi) | Clear Eos Tower floor 50 or kill Dogon |
| **KFT** (Lv 50-70) | Lv 60 top + bottom + shoes (Korean-themed) | Clear Nine-Tailed Fox chain |
| **OPQ** (Orbis, Lv 51-70) | PQ rewards, EXP | Party quest, 3-6 players |
| **Mu Lung Dojo** (any level) | Belt + shoulder scrolls (unique!) | Clear floor 25 |
| **Aqua Road** (Lv 50-70) | Lv 55 face acc + eye acc | "Depth Explorer" quest chain |
| **Herb Town PPQ** (Lv 55-70) | Lv 60 pirate weapon + gloves | Clear PPQ |
| **Zipangu** (Lv 60-80) | Lv 70 weapon + shoes (ninja) | Kill Bodyguard A |
| **Ariant** (Lv 60-80) | Lv 70 gloves + Lv 65 cape | Win 10 Colosseum matches |

**You don't need to do all of these.** Pick the ones that fill your empty gear slots. But exploring more = better gear across more slots.

**3rd job advancement at Lv 70** — El Nath instructor tests (solo).

### Level 70: Zakum

Your first real boss. The prequest is a **jump quest only**.

- **Party**: Solo-able with good gear, easier with 2-3
- **Reward**: **Zakum Helmet** — best hat until CZak Helm at 120. Scroll it. Love it.
- **Tip**: Bring lots of potions.

### Levels 70-100: Mid-Game

More zones open up, each with meaningful vendor gear:

| Zone | What You Get | How |
|------|-------------|-----|
| **Leafre** (Lv 50-70) | Lv 70 hat + overall (dragon) | Leafre dragon quest chain |
| **Magatia** (Lv 70-85) | Lv 78 faction weapon + unique potions | Faction quest — pick Alcadno OR Zenumist (different stats!) |
| **Kampung** (Lv 50-70) | **Scarlion Hat** (DEX/LUK) or **Targa Hat** (STR/INT) | Kill the boss. Buffed ZHelm alternatives. |
| **Singapore** (Lv 80-100) | Lv 85 earring + ring, accessory scrolls | Complete 4-step Singapore chain |
| **NLC** (Lv 70-100) | Lv 80 overall + cape (urban) | Clear elemental chambers |
| **ToT** (Lv 70-100) | Lv 90 weapon + pendant | Complete all gate stages (100 kills each) |

**Bosses**:
- **Krexel** (Lv 85+, 2-4 players) — Ulu City. Drops Krexel's Heart (unique accessory).
- **Pianus** (Lv 90+, duo) — Aqua Road deep sea.

### Level 100: Horntail + 4th Job

Big milestone. 4th job advancement.

- **Party**: 4-6 players recommended
- **Reward**: **Horntail Pendant** (amazing stats) + **Silver Blossom Ring**
- **Prequest**: Collect dragon eggs from Leafre
- **Tip**: Need a Bishop ideally. Bring All Cures.

### Levels 100-120: Corrupted Temple of Time

Each ToT zone requires 100 kills to advance. Push deeper for Corrupted mobs (Lv 110-120).

**Corrupted ToT completion vendor** (after 3-step chain): Lv 110 weapon + hat with dark glow effect + **Corrupted Scrolls** (70% success, no destroy on fail, dark visual).

### Level 120: Chaos Bosses

**Chaos Zakum** — 4-6 well-geared players. Reward: **CZak Helm** (upgrade from ZHelm).
**Chaos Horntail** — 6+ players. Reward: **CHT Pendant** (upgrade from HT pendant).

### Levels 120-130: Lion Heart Castle

Accessed from El Nath. Castle mobs drop **Von Leon Coins** — save these!

- Entry (Lv 120-122): Stumps, Boars, Guards
- Mid (Lv 122-126): Golems, Knights, Tower Anis
- Hard (Lv 126-130): Corrupted CK scouts

### Level 125: Von Leon

The lord of Lion Heart Castle.

- **Party**: 4-6 players
- **Reward**: **Von Leon Set** piece (random drop) + 10 Von Leon Coins
- **Vendor**: Trade Von Leon Coins for specific set pieces (~50 coins per piece). No bad luck — farm the coins, buy what you need.
- **Set bonus (3pc)**: +15 all stats, +300 HP/MP
- **Set bonus (6pc)**: +30 all stats, +500 HP/MP, +10 ATK/MATK

### Levels 130-140: Future Ereve

*Through the Gate to the Future. Ereve lies in ruins.*

Accessed from Temple of Time. CK Knight mobs drop **Soul Shards** — save these!

If you did the Ereve quests at Lv 20... every corrupted knight you fight is someone you knew.

**Mini-boss**: Guard Captain Darknell guards the approach to Empress.

### Level 140: Empress Cygnus

The ultimate fight. Three phases.

**Phase 1**: CK commanders one at a time (Mihile → Oz → Irena → Eckhart → Hawkeye)
**Phase 2**: Empowered versions of all five
**Phase 3**: Shinsoo + Empress Cygnus herself

- **Party**: 4-6 players, mixed classes, Bishop strongly recommended
- **Time**: 20-30 minutes with a good group
- **Reward**: **Empress Set** piece (random drop) + 3-5 Soul Shards
- **Vendor**: Trade Soul Shards for specific Empress pieces (10 shards per piece). ~3-5 runs for a guaranteed piece.
- **Set bonus (4pc)**: +25 all stats, +500 HP/MP, +10 ATK/MATK
- **Set bonus (7pc)**: +50 all stats, +1000 HP/MP, +20 ATK/MATK, +10% boss damage

### Level 140+: 4.5 Job Skills (Rien)

Defeating Empress unlocks the Rien Keystone. Travel to Rien for your **4.5 job trials** — 3 class-specific trials + 1 final challenge = **4 new skills** unique to your job.

Every final job gets a different kit. Examples:
- **Hero**: Soul Driver (ranged), Rolling Spin (AoE), Final Charge (gap close), Raging Tempest (ULTIMATE screen nuke)
- **Shadower**: Bloody Storm (6-hit combo), Tornado Spin (360° AoE), Mirror Image (shadow clone +50% DPS), Final Cut (ULTIMATE dash + 30s buff)
- **Bishop**: Sacred Pillar (AoE + heal), Holy Gear (party regen), Radiance (party EXP + holy buff), Divine Judgment (ULTIMATE full heal + 10s party invuln)

Full skill list: see [twinleaf-skills.md](twinleaf-skills.md)

**Rien vendor**: Skill Stones (2M meso, +1 skill level, max 3 per skill) + Lv 140 ring and earring.

### Levels 150-170: Arcane River (Arc II)

*The corruption is over. Now discover where magic comes from.*

Accessed via sealed portal in Rien after mastering all 4.5 skills. Three zones:
- **River's Edge** (150-157): Erda spirits test your intent
- **Deep Forest** (157-165): Nature run wild — Pinedeer, Wolfruit
- **River's Heart** (165-170): Elemental spirits, raw power

**Boss**: Arcane Guardian (Lv 170) — the ultimate endgame fight.
**Arcane Set**: Drops from zone mobs + boss. Token vendor for bad luck protection.

## Gear Priority Checklist

At each tier, this is what you want:

- [ ] **Lv 25-30**: Ereve quest rewards (pendant, gloves, scroll)
- [ ] **Lv 35-64**: Maple Weapons from Mushroom Castle vendor
- [ ] **Lv 50-60**: Fill empty slots from zone vendors (KFT armor, Ellin accessories, Omega hat)
- [ ] **Lv 70**: **Zakum Helmet** — scroll it!
- [ ] **Lv 70-80**: Magatia faction weapon OR Zipangu ninja weapon
- [ ] **Lv 80**: Scarlion/Targa Hat if your class wants different stats than ZHelm
- [ ] **Lv 85-90**: Singapore accessories, ToT weapon + pendant
- [ ] **Lv 100**: **Horntail Pendant + Ring**
- [ ] **Lv 110**: Corrupted ToT dark-tier gear + Corrupted Scrolls
- [ ] **Lv 120**: **CZak Helm** (direct ZHelm upgrade)
- [ ] **Lv 125**: **Von Leon Set** (boss drops + coin vendor)
- [ ] **Lv 140**: **Empress Set** (boss drops + shard vendor)
- [ ] **Lv 140+**: Rien accessories + max out 4.5 skills with Skill Stones
- [ ] **Lv 160+**: **Arcane Set** (boss + token vendor)

**Scrolling is king.** No Potential system. No Bonus Potential. Just you, scrolls, and luck. 60% scrolls are safe. 10% scrolls are powerful but risky. 30% scrolls are the sweet spot. Corrupted Scrolls (from Corrupted ToT) are 70% with no destroy — the friendliest scrolls in the game.

## The Vendor System

**Every zone you complete unlocks a vendor selling real gear.** You're never stuck relying on drop RNG for basic progression. Boss drops are the premium tier — exciting when they happen, but you can always buy "good enough" gear from vendors you've earned.

**Endgame sets use token currencies** for bad luck protection:
| Set | Token | Source | Exchange Rate |
|-----|-------|--------|---------------|
| Von Leon (Lv 125) | Von Leon Coins | LHC mobs (1-3) + Von Leon (10) | ~50 coins per piece |
| Empress (Lv 140) | Soul Shards | Future Ereve mobs (rare) + Empress (3-5) | 10 shards per piece |
| Arcane (Lv 160+) | Arcane Tokens | Arcane mobs + Guardian | TBD |
