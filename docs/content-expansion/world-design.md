# TwinleafStory — World Design & Coherence Blueprint

*The game MapleStory was supposed to be.*
*Last updated: 2026-02-28 — Session 3 (corrected with Windows repack audit data)*

---

## Design Philosophy

TwinleafStory is a **hybrid** — light narrative framing where it matters (Temple of Time, Future Ereve, Empress) but mostly a world that rewards exploration. Not every zone needs a cutscene. But every zone needs a **reason to exist** and a **reason to return**.

Four rules:
1. **Every town has a reason to visit at least once** — even if it's just a unique chair, a scroll vendor, or a quest chain that leads somewhere good.
2. **Rewards are the connective tissue** — gear, chairs, scrolls, skills, and cosmetics tie zones together. A scroll that drops in Singapore upgrades gear you got in Leafre. A chair from LHC proves you were there.
3. **The corruption is background texture, not a lecture** — NPCs mention strange things happening. ToT feels wrong. Future Ereve is the payoff. But you can ignore the story and still enjoy the game.
4. **Two macro arcs, one world** — the corruption arc (1-140) resolves at Empress. The source arc (140-170) is a new chapter. The tone shifts from dark/corrupted to elemental/awe. Rien is the bridge.

## Macro Story Structure (DECIDED 2026-02-28)

```
MACRO ARC I — THE CORRUPTION (Lv 1-140)
  Something is wrong with the world. Time is unstable. Familiar
  places are twisted. The CK Knights you befriended are falling.
  You stop it by defeating Empress Cygnus in the dark future.
  Climax: Empress Cygnus raid.
  Reward: 4.5 job skills via Rien, Ereve restored.

MACRO ARC II — THE SOURCE (Lv 140-170, Milestone 4)
  You stopped the corruption, but WHERE did it come from?
  Rien's sealed archives reveal: the Arcane River — the source
  of ALL magic in the world. The corruption was something trying
  to REACH the river from outside. Now you go there yourself.
  The river isn't evil — it's raw, untamed elemental power.
  Its guardians (Erda spirits) test anyone who enters.
  Gate: 4.5 job mastery (no new stat system).
  Climax: TBD (Arcane Guardian / River's Heart).
  Reward: Arcane gear tier, ultimate endgame.
```

The key insight: Arc I is about **saving what exists**. Arc II is about **discovering what's beyond**. The tone shifts from dread to wonder. The Erda spirits aren't corrupted — they're ancient, elemental, and indifferent to your problems. You're not a hero here, you're an explorer again.

## Area-by-Area Design

### Maple Island & Victoria Island (Lv 1-30)
**Status**: Stock v83, no changes needed.
**Role**: Tutorial and early exploration. Every class starts here.
**Coherence**: Standard. Cody NPC in towns gives level-appropriate guidance.

### Ereve — The Knights' Island (Lv 20-50, accessible early)
**Status**: REPURPOSED. CK character creation disabled. Island open to Explorers.
**Access**: Boat from Ellinia (stock v83 route).
**Role**: Peaceful training island + lore foundation. Players meet CK NPCs (Mihile, Oz, Irena, Eckhart, Hawkeye) who exist as friendly quest-givers, not class trainers.

**Why it matters later**: Every NPC you meet here shows up as a corrupted mob in Future Ereve (Act 4). Dawn, Blaze, Wind, Night, Thunder mobs ARE these knights, fallen. If you visited Ereve early, Future Ereve hits different.

**Rewards**:
- Low-level CK-themed cosmetic gear (repurposed CK equips as Explorer-wearable)
- Unique chairs (Empress Waiting Room chair, Knight's Rest chair)
- Introductory scroll quests (Ereve vendors sell basic scrolls not available elsewhere)
- Lore journal entries (collectible, feeds into Rien questline later)

**Quest hooks**:
- Mihile asks you to report on the state of the world (recurring quest, returns at each act)
- Hawkeye gives a quest chain about sea routes (teaches players about boats/travel)
- Oz hints that the Temple of Time is "acting strange" (plants Act 3 seed)

### Ossyria / El Nath / Leafre / Mu Lung / Aqua Road (Lv 30-70)
**Status**: Stock v83.
**Role**: Core mid-game. Standard progression.
**Coherence**: Cody NPC appears in El Nath and Leafre. Each area has unique reward reasons:
- **El Nath**: Gateway to LHC later. Dead Mine → Zakum.
- **Leafre**: Dragon-themed gear. Gateway to ToT.
- **Mu Lung**: Dojo (timed challenge, unique rewards — chairs, scrolls).
- **Aqua Road**: Underrated. Could stock unique scrolls or a hidden chair here.

**Zakum (Lv 70)** — First real boss. Helm is still BiS for a while. Chair drop. Entry to "boss culture."

### Omega Sector (Lv 45-65, parallel zone)
**Status**: AVAILABLE (123 maps in repack, 221000000-221040402).
**Access**: Stock v83 — Eos Tower connects Ludibrium ↔ Omega Sector.
**Role**: Parallel leveling zone for a change of scenery. Sci-fi/robot aesthetic is unique in the game. Not mandatory progression — just variety.

**Key features**:
- **Eos Tower** (101 floors): Vertical progression challenge, natural dojo-like experience
- **Boswell/Kulan Fields**: Open grinding fields with robot mob evolution chain (Roid → Neo Huroid → Frankenroid)
- **Drummer Bunny** boss (25-map lair): Repeatable boss encounter
- **Dogon** (HQ): Area boss

**Priority**: Milestone 2+. Content exists and is ready to enable — just needs mob stat tuning and drop tables.

### Kampung Village (Lv 50-70, boss hat zone)
**Status**: AVAILABLE (28 maps in repack, 551000000-551030200).
**Access**: Connected to Singapore area.
**Role**: Boss hat zone — Scarlion and Targa fights with unique helmet rewards.

**Completion vendor**: Kill Scarlion OR Targa → unlock vendor selling:
- **Scarlion Hat** — BUFFED. Lv 80, DEX/LUK focus. ZHelm alternative for thieves/bowmen.
- **Targa Hat** — BUFFED. Lv 80, STR/INT focus. ZHelm alternative for warriors/mages.

**Priority**: Low. Enable when Singapore is working since they share the travel route.

**Role**: Alternative grinding zone with elemental chamber challenges.

**Completion vendor**: Clear elemental chambers → unlock vendor selling:
- NLC gear (Lv 80 overall + cape, urban aesthetic)

### Amoria (Any level)
**Status**: Stock v83. Wedding town.
**Role**: Social content. Weddings = unique equip reward.

**Completion vendor**: Get married → unlock:
- **Wedding Ring** (+2 all stats, untradeable, unique equip)
- Couple cosmetics (matching outfits)

### Singapore / Ulu City (Lv 80-100)
**Status**: NEW (import from repack). 30 maps, era-appropriate GMS content.
**Access**: NPC at Changi Airport (stock v83 Singapore travel exists in-game).
**Role**: Alternative grinding path for 80-100. Fills the gap where players currently loop Leafre.

**Why it matters**: Variety. Players who grind only Leafre burn out. Singapore is a whole different aesthetic — urban, tropical, foreign. Krexel (giant tree boss) is a unique multi-body fight like Zakum but for the 85-90 range.

**Rewards**:
- Singapore Face Mask (accessory, already in repack equips)
- Krexel unique drops (gear + chair)
- Singapore-exclusive scrolls (the 11 new consume items — check if any fit here)
- Travel achievement (Cody acknowledges you've been abroad)

### Temple of Time — Entry (Lv 70-100)
**Status**: Stock v83 + kill quest reduction (999 → 100 per stage).
**Role**: The narrative spine. This is where the story lives — you're walking through the past.
**Coherence**: Each stage gate (100 kills instead of 999) still feels like a progression check but doesn't feel like punishment. The NPCs here hint at corruption ("the memories are becoming unstable").

### Corrupted Temple of Time (Lv 100-120)
**Status**: NEW (6 corrupted mob variants from repack: Wyvern, Cornian, Skelegon, Kentaurus, Chief Memory Guardian, Time Monk Trainee).
**Access**: Deeper ToT maps. After completing the standard ToT stages.
**Role**: Bridge content. Makes ToT relevant beyond 100. The corruption is now visible — familiar mobs are twisted.

**Why it matters**: This is the first sign that something is actually wrong. It's not just "harder mobs." The NPCs acknowledge that these corrupted versions shouldn't exist. Plants the seed for Future Ereve.

**Rewards**:
- Corrupted versions of existing equipment (dark reskins, slightly better stats)
- Unique scrolls (Corrupted Scroll of X — higher success rate but adds a dark visual effect?)
- Corrupted Chair (trophy, dark-themed)

### Lion Heart Castle (Lv 120-130)
**Status**: NEW (import from repack). 28 maps + mobs from El Nath mountains.
**Access**: New NPC/portal in El Nath. Story hook: a CK scouting party went into the mountains investigating reports of a dark castle. They never came back.

**Mob roster (76 mobs across 3 groups):**
- **Castle mobs (8210000-8210013, 14 mobs)**: Crocky the Gatekeeper, Reindeer, Blood Reindeer, Bearwolf, Grey Vulture, Castle Golem, Prison Guards (Boar/Rhino/Ani), Tower Ani variants
- **CK Knight enemies (8610000-8610033, 34 mobs)**: Dawn/Blaze/Wind/Night/Thunder (corrupted CK forms), Official Knights A-G, Advanced Knights A-E, Ifrit, Watchmen
- **Forest/ruin mobs (8620000-8620027, 28 mobs)**: Swollen Stumps, Pillaging Boars, Sinister Masks, Ancient Golems

**Why it matters**: First major "new" area. El Nath → LHC gives El Nath a reason to revisit. The castle theme contrasts with the icy mountains. Von Leon at 125 is a real gear-check boss.

**Rewards**:
- Von Leon equipment set (Lv 125 tier — slots between Zakum Helm and Empress)
- LHC-exclusive chairs (Castle Throne, Prison Chair, etc.)
- Von Leon drops scrolls for the new gear slots (belt, pendant, shoulder if applicable)
- LHC mob drops: crafting materials → Von Leon gear recipes? Or straight drops.

### Chaos Zakum / Chaos Horntail (Lv 120)
**Status**: NEW (17 Chaos Zakum mobs from repack). Chaos HT may already exist in server.
**Role**: Hard-mode revisits of familiar bosses. You already beat Zakum at 70 — now it's corrupted and 10x harder.

**Coherence**: These aren't random "hard modes." In the corruption narrative, Zakum and Horntail are being twisted by the same force affecting ToT. The NPCs in the boss entry maps could have new dialogue about the corruption.

**Rewards**:
- Chaos Zakum Helm (upgrade from normal Zakum Helm)
- Chaos-tier equipment drops
- Unique chairs (Chaos Zakum Arm Chair — sitting on a dismembered boss arm)

### Future Ereve / Gate to the Future (Lv 130-140)
**Status**: NEW (42 maps from repack). Accessed from Temple of Time.
**Role**: THE narrative climax zone. You step through a time portal and see Ereve — the island you visited in Act 1 — destroyed. The CK Knights are corrupted enemies.

**Mob roster (~100 mobs across two groups):**
- **CK Knight enemies (8610000-8610004, 5 base + higher-level variants)**: Dawn, Blaze, Wind, Night, Thunder Warriors — corrupted versions of the Cygnus Knights from Ereve Act 1. Same mobs as LHC but at higher levels (130-140), representing the corruption spreading. The narrative gut-punch: these are the knights who trained you.
- **Encounter area mobs (8645000-8645065, ~63 mobs)**: Cygnus/Nova/Resistance Support Troops (wave fodder), Corrupted Warrior, Black Knight, Mad Mage, Rampant Cyborg, Vicious Hunter, Bad Brawler, Official Knights, Advanced Knights — the ruined Ereve garrison.
- **Guard Captain Darknell (8645009)**: Mini-boss gating the CK commander fights. Encountered between the general Future Ereve grinding and the Empress encounter itself.

Note: The Erda spirits (8641xxx), Forest mobs (8642xxx), Elemental spirits (8644xxx), and Event/dream mobs (8643xxx) from the repack are **Arcane River mobs** (see Arcane River section below), NOT Future Ereve mobs. They share a similar ID prefix range but belong to completely different zones narratively and geographically.

**Why it matters**: If the player visited Ereve in Act 1 and met these NPCs, this is devastating. Even without the story investment, it's a visually distinct area with high-level mobs.

**Rewards**:
- Pre-Empress gear (bridge between Von Leon and Empress sets)
- Future Ereve chairs (Ruined Throne, Corrupted Knight's Rest — dark mirrors of Ereve chairs)
- Key item: "Vision Fragment" or similar — needed for Empress entry
- Corrupted CK skill books? (preview of 4.5 job — you find fragments of their techniques)

### Empress Cygnus (Lv 140) — The Ultimate Boss
**Status**: NEW. Full encounter data confirmed in repack:

**Encounter Area mobs (8645000-8645065, ~63 mobs):**
- Cygnus Support Troops, Nova Support Troops, Resistance Support Troops (wave fodder)
- Corrupted Warrior, Black Knight, Mad Mage, Rampant Cyborg, Vicious Hunter, Bad Brawler
- Guard Captain Darknell (mini-boss, gates the CK commander fights)

**CK Commander bosses (8850000-8850011, 12 mobs):**
| Phase | Bosses |
|-------|--------|
| Phase 1 | Mihile (8850000), Oz (8850001), Irena (8850002), Eckhart (8850003), Hawkeye (8850004) |
| Phase 2 | Mihile (8850005), Oz (8850006), Irena (8850007), Eckhart (8850008), Hawkeye (8850009) |
| Final | Shinsoo (8850010, sacred bird), **Cygnus herself (8850011)** |

**The narrative payoff**: Every commander you fight is an NPC you met in Ereve during Act 1. Mihile who gave you sword training is now trying to kill you. Oz who taught you about magic is casting dark spells. This is the emotional climax of the corruption arc — you're saving the people who helped you.

**Role**: Multi-phase raid. The hardest content in the game until Arcane River. Minimum 4 players recommended, 4-6 ideal. Solo possible but extremely difficult.

**Rewards**:
- Empress equipment set (Lv 140, BiS until Arcane)
- Empress Chair (ultimate trophy)
- 4.5 Job unlock quest trigger (defeating Empress starts the questline)
- Title/cosmetic effect (visual proof you cleared the endgame)
- Rare: Shinsoo mount? (if mount data exists in repack)

### Rien — The Frozen Archive (Lv 140+, post-Empress)
**Status**: REPURPOSED. Aran creation disabled. Island reimagined as endgame content.
**Access**: Unlocked after defeating Empress Cygnus. Boat from Lith Harbor (stock route).
**Role**: 4.5 Job preparation zone + Arcane River backstory.

**Design concept**: Rien is a frozen island where the ancient hero Aran sealed away knowledge of advanced combat techniques. After proving yourself against Empress, you're worthy to access the archive.

**4.5 Job questline**:
1. Defeat Empress → receive "Rien Keystone"
2. Travel to Rien → meet the Archive Keeper NPC
3. Class-specific trial dungeon (solo instance, tests mastery of your current skills)
4. Unlock 3-4 new skills per Explorer branch (sourced from CK/Aran skill animations)
5. Each skill has a quest to "master" it (use it X times, upgrade its level)

**Arcane River foreshadowing**:
- Rien NPCs talk about "what lies beyond the frozen sea" — the Arcane River
- Lore tablets describe a power source that transcends the five elements (CK elements)
- A sealed portal exists that "responds to no known magic" — future M4 content hook

**Rewards**:
- 4.5 Job skills (3-4 per Explorer branch, sourced from 333 CK/Aran skill pool)
- Rien-exclusive gear (Lv 140+ accessories, small upgrades)
- Rien chairs (Frozen Throne, Archive Chair, Aran's Rest)
- Cosmetic: "Legacy of Rien" title or aura effect

### Chaos Horntail (Lv 120)
**Status**: No separate mob IDs in repack. Likely uses same IDs as regular HT with stat overrides. Additional HT summons exist (8810019-8810026: Red/Blue/Dark Wyvern, Green/Dark Cornian, Summon Horntail heads).

### Arcane River (Milestone 4, Lv 150-170)
**Status**: FUTURE. 379 maps (Map4 directory), 300+ mobs in repack. Phased import.
**Access**: Sealed portal in Rien. Opens when ALL 4.5 job skill quests are mastered.
**Role**: Arc II content. True endgame. The source of all magic.

**Narrative**: The Arcane River is where raw magic flows. It's not corrupted — it's primordial, elemental, indifferent. The Erda spirits are its consciousness, and they test anyone who enters. You're not saving the world here — you're exploring the deepest secret of the world. The tone is wonder and challenge, not dread.

**No Black Mage references.** All NPC dialogue and quest text is custom. The river exists because magic has to come from somewhere.

**Three zones (phased import):**

| Zone | Level | Maps | Mobs | Theme |
|------|-------|------|------|-------|
| River's Edge | 150-157 | ~40 (first Vanishing Journey equiv) | Erda spirits (8641xxx) | Introduction — calm, mysterious, spirits probe your intent |
| Deep Forest | 157-165 | ~40 (Chu Chu equiv) | Forest mobs (8642xxx) | Nature run wild — Pinedeer, Ewenana, Wolfruit |
| River's Heart | 165-170 | ~40 (Lachelein equiv) | Elemental spirits (8644xxx) | Raw power — Water/Sun/Earth/Thunder spirits |

**Gating between zones (DECIDED: 4.5 mastery, not Arcane Force):**
- Enter River's Edge: Complete all 4.5 job skill quests (Rien)
- Enter Deep Forest: Complete River's Edge quest chain + level 157
- Enter River's Heart: Complete Deep Forest chain + Empress gear equipped + level 165

**Mob stat rewrite**: All mobs re-statted for pre-BB damage formulas. At 150-170 with Empress gear and 4.5 skills, difficulty should feel like Future Ereve scaled up — hard but fair.

**Boss**: Arcane Guardian at the River's Heart (TBD design — could be a multi-phase elemental boss using the spirit mob models).

**Gear**: Arcane Set (Lv 160-170, replaces Empress as BiS). Drops from zone mobs and the Arcane Guardian.

**What we skip**: The remaining ~260 maps (Arcana, Morass, Esfera equivalents) are future expansion if the player base wants more. Three zones is plenty for launch.

## Reward Design — How Everything Connects

### Gear Ladder — Vendor + Boss Combined (DECIDED 2026-02-28)

Every zone has a **completion vendor** selling level-appropriate gear. Boss drops are the premium upgrade on top. Players are never stuck — there's always a deterministic path to "good enough" gear, with RNG drops as exciting bonuses.

**Principle**: Zone vendor gear = reliable baseline (~70-80% as strong as boss drops). Boss drops = premium tier. Token currencies for endgame sets provide bad luck protection.

```
Lv 1-15:   Stock NPC gear (trash)
Lv 15-25:  Victoria completion → basic adventurer set
Lv 25-30:  Ereve quests → Knight Trainee accessories + gloves
Lv 35-64:  Mushroom Castle → MAPLE WEAPONS (iconic, earned)
Lv 50-60:  KFT / Omega Sector / Ellin → mid-tier armor + accessories
Lv 60-70:  Zipangu / Ariant / Herb Town → pre-boss weapons + gloves + capes
Lv 70:     ZAKUM HELM (boss drop, BiS hat for ages)
Lv 70-80:  Leafre / Magatia → Lv 70-78 gear, faction weapons
Lv 80:     Scarlion/Targa HATS (boss, ZHelm stat alternatives)
Lv 80-90:  Singapore / NLC / ToT → Lv 85-90 accessories + armor
Lv 90-100: Krexel Heart (boss quest), HT Pendant (boss drop)
Lv 100-110: Corrupted ToT → dark-tier gear + corrupted scrolls
Lv 120:    CZak Helm (boss), CHT Pendant (boss)
Lv 120-125: LHC → VON LEON SET (boss drops + vendor via Von Leon Coins)
Lv 130-140: Future Ereve → EMPRESS SET (boss drops + vendor via Soul Shards)
Lv 140:    Rien → endgame accessories + Skill Stones
Lv 150-170: Arcane River → ARCANE SET (boss + vendor via Arcane Tokens)
```

### Cash Shop Philosophy (DECIDED 2026-02-28)

**NX is for cosmetics. NX is normal. NX is not P2W.**

The cash shop exists because cosmetic monetization is how MapleStory works — and players enjoy it. The rules:

1. **No gacha. No random boxes. No Royal Hair. No surprise mechanics. Ever.** You see what you're buying. You buy what you see.
2. **No pay-to-win.** No EXP coupons, no 2x drop cards, no stat-boosting items in the cash shop. Power comes from gameplay.
3. **NX buys looks, not power.** Premium cosmetic equipment, pet skins, aura effects, chat effects — all fine. These are the fun part.
4. **Meso alternative for everything.** Every cosmetic in the cash shop can also be earned via meso (higher price, but achievable). NX is the convenience/premium path, not the only path.
5. **Some NX-exclusive cosmetics are OK.** Having a few premium looks that are NX-only is fine — that's the point of a premium currency. But never NX-exclusive power.
6. **Rotating monthly stock.** The cash shop updates monthly with new cosmetic items. FOMO is OK for cosmetics ("this outfit is only available this month"). Not OK for power.
7. **Quest-reward cosmetics can't be bought.** Some of the best-looking gear comes from quests and achievements. You can't buy the "Corruption's End" aura. You have to earn it.

#### Tier 3 — Secret Quests (~5 chains)

No quest markers. No NPC with a lightbulb. These quests are triggered by world interaction — entering a specific map at a specific time, clicking an object nobody notices, or having a specific item in your inventory.

| Quest | Trigger | Steps | Reward |
|-------|---------|-------|--------|
| **The Broken Compass** | Find 1 of 5 compass fragments (rare mob drops in 5 different zones) | Collect all 5 → combine at a hidden workbench in Lith Harbor → compass points to a hidden map | Hidden map + unique chair + "Cartographer" medal |

**Purpose**: The ultimate exploration reward. Players who pay attention to the world, experiment with timing, and follow cryptic clues get unique rewards that prove their dedication.

#### Tier 4 — Server Secrets (1-2)

Easter eggs. Not documented. Not hinted at. Placed in the game and waited on. If a player finds one, it becomes legendary. We don't design these publicly — they're placed during implementation and left for discovery.

**Design rules for Tier 4**:
- Must be technically possible to find (no impossible triggers)
- Reward should be unique and memorable (a one-of-a-kind chair? A secret title?)
- No gameplay advantage — pure bragging rights
- If nobody finds it in 6 months, drop a subtle hint via Cassandra

#### Exploration Support Item

**Adventurer's Maps** — sold by the Lith Harbor vendor:
- Cost: 20k meso
- Effect: Reveals hidden portal locations on the minimap for 30 minutes
- Scope: Current map + adjacent maps
- Helps players find Tier 1 scenic secrets and Tier 2 hidden vendor entrances
- Does NOT reveal Tier 3/4 content (those require real discovery)

## Returning NPCs — Familiar Faces with Real Roles (DECIDED 2026-02-28)

**Stock role**: Event herald. Players ignore her.
**TwinleafStory role**: Ambient storytelling engine. Appears in all major towns (stock spawn locations).

**Mechanics**:

- **No quests of her own.** She points, she doesn't assign.

**Personality**: Nosy, chatty, dramatic. Treats everything like gossip. "Don't tell anyone I told you this, but..."

### Maple Admin — Server's Voice & QoL Vendor

**Stock role**: GM placeholder, announces server events.
**TwinleafStory role**: Meta-commentary NPC and server admin interface. Appears in **trade lobby only**.

**Mechanics**:

**Personality**: Self-aware, tired, helpful. IT helpdesk guy who's actually good at his job.

### Gaga — Collection Tracker & Milestone Rewards

**Stock role**: Pointless low-level fetch quests nobody remembers.
**TwinleafStory role**: Chair and medal collector NPC. Located in Henesys (stock location).

**Mechanics**:

  - 100 chairs → "Master Collector" medal + "Gaga's Dream Chair" (golden toilet)

**Personality**: Excitable, cowardly, endearing. Hero-worships the player. "You went WHERE?! And came back ALIVE?!"

### Duey — Player-to-Player Mail System

**Stock role**: Cash shop delivery. Barely used on private servers.

**Mechanics**:

## Equipment Model Strategy (DECIDED 2026-02-28)

### The Problem
Von Leon and Empress equipment sets have **string/name data** in the repack but **visual models are mostly missing** from Character.wz. Players would see invisible gear.

### The Solution: Reskin Redundant Gear

Rather than sourcing external WZ data (version mismatch risk, legal gray area), **reskin existing v83 equipment** that's redundant or underused:

1. **Identify redundant high-level gear** in the repack (duplicate models, underused equips)
2. **Reassign item IDs** — point Von Leon/Empress item IDs to existing visual models
3. **Custom names via String.wz** — the string data already exists, just map it to the reskinned models
4. **Custom stats via server** — Cosmic handles stats server-side, independent of client visuals

This means Von Leon gear might visually look like "dark knight armor" using an existing model, and Empress gear uses a different existing model — but the names, stats, and set bonuses are all custom TwinleafStory.

### Fallback
If a fuller v83-compatible WZ extract becomes available, swap in the real models later. The server-side stats/names don't change — only the client-side visual model reference.

### Scarlion/Targa Hats (Kampung)
- **Already in repack** (Kampung content is present)
- **Buff stats** to make them competitive alternatives to Zakum Helm:
  - Scarlion Hat: Lv 80, DEX/LUK focus (thief/bowman alternative)
  - Targa Hat: Lv 80, STR/INT focus (warrior/mage alternative)
- Gives Kampung a clear identity: "boss hats with different stat spreads than ZHelm"
